What elements would go in our model?

Dissipation:

  • motor/gearbox rotational damping $b$
  • motor electrical resistance $R$

Storage

  • motor/gearbox rotational kinetic energy stored in rotational inertia $J$
  • Translational kinetic energy stored in Romi total mass $m$

Transduction

  • Electrical-Rotational Mechanical transducer in motor $k_t$
  • Rotational-Translational Mechanical transducer from wheels with radius $r$

What are the elemental, loop and node equations for this circuit?

Nodes \begin{equation} i = i \end{equation} \begin{equation} T_m = T_b+T_J+T_r \end{equation} \begin{equation} F_r=F_m \end{equation}

Loops \begin{equation} V_{1g} = V_{12}+V_{2g} \end{equation}

Elements \begin{equation} V_{12}=iR \end{equation} \begin{equation} k_t i = T_m \end{equation} \begin{equation} k_t \Omega_{1g} = V_{2g} \end{equation} \begin{equation} T_b = b\Omega_{1g} \end{equation} \begin{equation} T_J = J\dot{\Omega}_{1g} \end{equation} \begin{equation} T_r = rF_r \end{equation} \begin{equation} v_{1g} = r\Omega_{1g} \end{equation} \begin{equation} F_m = m\dot{v}_{1g} \end{equation}
